Research & Innovation

Pushing the boundaries of VLSI design, embedded systems, and semiconductor technology

Focus Areas

Research Areas

Our faculty actively research across these domains, contributing to IEEE, Springer, and Elsevier journals.

Low-Power VLSI Design

Research into ultra-low-power circuit design techniques, power gating, clock gating, and energy-efficient architectures for IoT and mobile applications.

Power GatingClock TreeFinFET

System-on-Chip (SoC) Design

End-to-end SoC design methodology including IP integration, bus architecture, DFT, and verification using UVM and SystemVerilog.

AMBAUVMDFT

FPGA Design & Prototyping

Rapid prototyping on Xilinx and Intel FPGAs. Research in reconfigurable computing, HLS, and FPGA-based accelerators.

XilinxVivadoHLS

Analog & Mixed-Signal IC

Design and analysis of analog circuits, ADCs/DACs, PLLs, LDOs, and mixed-signal integration using Cadence Virtuoso.

PLLADCCadence

Nanoelectronics

Research in sub-nm transistor technologies, CNTFETs, quantum-dot devices, and emerging memory technologies like ReRAM and MRAM.

CNTFETReRAMMRAM

IoT & Embedded Systems

Edge computing, wireless sensor networks, LoRa/LoRaWAN, RISC-V based IoT nodes, and smart agriculture/healthcare applications.

RISC-VLoRaEdge AI
